v.1

-combat is based on trying to land a hit and faking out the opponent and dashing
-basic punch:
	short dash, quick activation, short active frames, small recovery, small whiff
-dash punch:
	longer dash, longer activation,  longer active frames, small recovery, medium whiff
-throw kunai:
	interrupt anything, limited supply
-perfect block:
	stagger enemy, catch enemy kunai throw back, costs chakra
